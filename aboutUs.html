Creating a Rescue and Adoption for Pets Marlianti, Arifah, and Hazirah worked together to create a haven for our four-legged friends right in the middle of our neighborhood. Driven by a common interest, we opened this website for pet adoption and rescue that is a virtual sanctuary with endearing narratives and visually appealing photos. All people who support giving abandoned and abused animals and dogs a second opportunity are welcome to enter our virtual sanctuary through this door.

This website is an appeal for kindness, asking kind-hearted people to make a cash donation to support the well-being of these animals. Through volunteer opportunities, it invites people to become the center of the shelter. We all three understand the value of providing comprehensive treatment, which makes working with nearby veterinary clinics possible. You are cordially encouraged to participate in this compassionate symphony while you peruse the website. Each donation, volunteer effort, and cooperative effort contributes to a melodious tune that echoes the organization's original vision of a world in which every foot finds rest and every heart finds meaning. Greetings from the sanctuary, where love has no limits.
